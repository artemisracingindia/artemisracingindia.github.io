What we do

We Artemis Racing India are official M-Baja team of SAE CLUB of VIVA Institute of Technology, Virar We are team of 18 members of undergraduate mechanical department of VIT and we design and fabricate an All-Terrain Vehicle from scratch..

We participate in National Level offroading competitions like BAJA SAEINDIA and MEGAATV CHAMPIONSHIP etc. Our goal is to aqcuire various technical, marketing skills and to provide hands-on experience to undergraduate students .
